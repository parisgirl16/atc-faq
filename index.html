I sent the document to the printer, now how do I print it?

Scan your SLU ID or input your Banner ID at any of the three printers on the first floor of Pius Library to print your document.

I couldn't get the printer to connect to my device. Are there other ways to print?

Yes! Upload your document and send it to Blueprint_Secure_Print at this website.

Where do I go to print posters, flyers, 3D prints, etc?

Come to the Print Shop on the first floor of Pius Library. Take a left before the elevator and go through the opening between the Innovation Studio and the wall with the large screen.
